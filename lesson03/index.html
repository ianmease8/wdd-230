Purpose

Domain Name:

southkingstownchamberofcommerce.org

the purpose for this website is to be a mockup of a chamber of commerce of my local town in rhode island. A place where buisnesses can learn about what permits apply to them and how quickly they can get them.

Target Audience

Persona 1

Business

This would be a person seeking to open up a business within the community and is seeking to the know the hours of operation to be able to insure that they can obtain the proper permits for their endeavors

Persona 2

Tourist

this would be tourists coming in to the area looking to see what businesses are in the area and which ones they want to stop by and see.

Senarios

  • a prospective businesswomen wants to see if the town will let her build her business
  • a business student looking to see what applicable laws and permits apply to the local businesses
  • a local resident looking to see if there are any events taking place
  • a business looking to advertise their local sales event
  • a bar looking to see if they can obtain another liquor liscense for another location
